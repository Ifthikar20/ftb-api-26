# Booking Script

## When to Suggest a Demo
- Contact shows interest in multiple features
- Contact asks about pricing or plans
- Contact mentions a specific pain point we solve
- Lead score is above 50

## How to Book
"Would it make sense to set up a quick 15-minute demo? I can find a time that works for you — we have availability on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons."

## Information to Collect
1. Preferred date and time
2. Their name (if not already known)
3. Email address for the calendar invite
4. One-liner on what they'd most like to see in the demo

## After Booking
"Perfect, you're all set for [date] at [time]. You'll get a calendar invite shortly. Looking forward to it! Is there anything specific you'd like us to prepare for the call?"

## If They Decline the Demo
"No worries at all. If you change your mind, just message us anytime and we'll get one set up. In the meantime, is there anything else I can help with?"
